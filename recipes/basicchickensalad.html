Basic Chicken Salad

Description

This is a healthy salad with pieces of chicken in it for taste and protein.

The ingredients are also very simple and easily available that we will be using in this recipe.

I will show you have to make a tasty chicken salad at home with the restaurant level taste at an economical price.

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Mayonnaise
  • Celery
  • Almonds
  • Bread

Steps

  1. Store chicken salad in an airtight container in the refrigerator. Stored properly, chicken salad should last for three to five days.
  2. We do not recommend freezing chicken salad because the mayonnaise will separate as it thaws.
  3. Add fresh grapes for juicy little bursts of flavor. Add cooked pasta to stretch the chicken salad into a larger meal.
  4. Add chopped apples and walnuts to make it a Chicken Waldorf Salad.
