Chicken Pot Pie

Description

This is a good chicken recipe that you should know.

This is a family favorite dish that has many fans in the family.

Make the best chicken pot pie of your life!

Ingredients

  • Chicken
  • Vegetables
  • Butter and Flour
  • Seasoning
  • Pie Crusts

Steps

  1. Boil the cubed chicken with the carrots, peas, and celery for about 15 minutes. Drain and set aside.
  2. Place the chicken and Vegetables mixture in a translucent pot.
  3. Allow the pie to cool completely before storage. Wrap the cooled pie tightly in aluminum foil and refrigerate for three to five days.
  4. Wrap the cooled pie tightly in aluminum foil and refrigerate for three to five days. Reheat in the oven or in short bursts in the microwave.
